| [Conversion Strategy Gate](./features/conversion-strategy.md) | Free | Define tracking objectives and get CONFIRM/AUGMENT/REPLACE verdicts |

| [Naming Conventions](./features/naming-conventions.md) | Free | Org-level event and parameter naming rules |
